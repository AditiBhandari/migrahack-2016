Many’s daughters. Photo by Many Uch.

In 2010, Many was granted a pardon by Washington State Governor Chris Gregoire, and in 2016 he was appointed to serve on the Washington Statewide Reentry Council. However, the 1996 laws that made his deportation automatic meant that this pardon and his government service do not necessarily cancel his deportation. He remains on “the list” to be deported to Cambodia. He does not know if or when he could be taken from his wife and young daughters.
